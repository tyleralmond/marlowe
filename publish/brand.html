MARLOWE was born in response to an emerging need created by the modern woman’s lifestyle and values. A heightened sense of what is important brings a quest for value that is defined by quality, a strength achieved through character integrity, and the independence of the intellectual spirit.

This foundation creates a focused collection with understated clean lines allowing the character the freedom of full expression.

The marriage of high quality cashmere knitwear with bespoke finely tailored sportswear makes MARLOWE unique in the world of fashion. The versatility of these combinations renders wardrobe building effortless.

The finest ingredients are used, from the highest spun wool fabrics to the finest cashmere. This is the core of the product. The best workmanship attained through the artisanry of italian manufacturing renders the product complete. A team in Italy devoted to providing the highest quality and attention to every detail that attracts the very client MARLOWE was created for.

Cashmere is known to be the fiber of nobles. But it is the nobility of the elegant woman that dictates the use of this most precious fiber. Second skin weights have provided a truly seasonless experience.

Chic and sophisticated with the ultimate in comfort and fit defines the brand’s pedigree.

Timeless in our interpretation, modern in our classicism, and in tune with the intrinsic values of the client.

Investment dressing that at once becomes an expression of quality.
